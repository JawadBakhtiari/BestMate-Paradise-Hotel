BestMate Paradise Hotel

BestMate has been around for 10 years and has worked in the local area of Sydney. This hotel aims to provide a safe space and care for any pets that are in need of a home while the owners are absent. We aim to make a connection with our owners by performing requirement analysis to assist in creating a suitable package, using membership to make personal connections. We allow almost all ranges of pets to stay in this hotel containing areas of different environment for various pets.

Staff

Our staff are warm, friendly and hospitable people who to love work with all types of pets. They are professionals with exemplar experience with working in other animal sancturaries. These staff aim to keep your furry friends happy and cared for. Our staff are all close and are eager to answer any questions or querries that you have about your pets stay or anything admin questions about this organisation.

Customers

Our use of membership levels ensure customers have authority over their personal account. Customers have the ability to adjust their booking when adding more pets or mroe requirements for their pets. This website helps create this connection automatically, allowing the staff to receive all requirements and be updated before the pet comes into the hotel, facillitating the process for the customers, pets and staff.
